Privacy Policy

In a nutshell

Pomodoro Timer does not collect or share any kind of personal or non-personal information.

Longer Version

  • We are strongly committed to protecting the privacy of the app's user community.
  • When you use this app, we know nothing about you, not even your BB PIN, or phone's IMEI number.
  • Any calculation or processing done while the app is running does not leave your phone memory, and are also not stored in the phone after the app is closed.
  • The only case when we may have some information about you is when you choose to leave us a feedback email. In that case, we obviously receive your email-id, as well as whatever content the mail body contains. However, we absolutely do not put that email-id into any use.
